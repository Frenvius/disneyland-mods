# Bookcase Mods for Constructed Kingdoms Dedicated Server. Mod made by Raelaziel.
Normal & Dark Wood Variations. 4 Options to slide left or right like a secret passageway! Also stationary.
Adds a Doors and Piece tab. Door for secret openings. Piece for normal.
